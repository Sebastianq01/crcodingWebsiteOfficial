Algebra 2: The Foundation for Computational Thought

Algebra 2 is considered the basic foudnation to advanced mathamatic learning for many high school students, as it incorporates many key terms, concepts and formulas that are integration to learning beyond the subject matter. This course will go in depth on how functions work and ways to manipulate graphs and data sets.

What do I need to learn Algebra 2?

Much of Algebra 2 can be simplified with teh use of a graphing calculator, which enables students to put in equations and plot desired graphs that correlate with the current lesson being covered. You can find a list of TI products here.

Online Tools and Resources

Many students find it helpful to use online tools to help map out their train of thought, particularly using online graphing sites or equation lists in PDFs. You can find Desmos, a powerful online graphing tool, by clicking here, and a list of helpful equations and descriptions here.
